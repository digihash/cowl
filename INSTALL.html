Installing Cowl

Welcome to Cowl,

We at the Cowl team are completely stoked to have you try out our awesome framework. Our aim is not to create the world's easiest-to-install framework, but instead something that works great. You will only install your framework once.

(Note)

If you are on a UNIX-like operating system you can use the "install"-file in the base directory. Just run it in the terminal and answer the very few questions you are asked. It does exactly the things in the checklist.

$ ./install
*** Installing Cowl.
** Moving ...
** ...
# Please enter the base web path of your app (almost always /):
/
*** Done.

Before

That being said, Cowl is easy to install. If you downloaded it off Github, you will probably have the following file-structure:

  • www-folder/
    • cowl/ (you are here)
      • .git/ (the .git-directory for Cowl)
      • app-SAMPLE
        • front/
        • back/
        • static/
      • index.php-SAMPLE
      • SAMPLE.htaccess
      • Other cowl-related files and directories

Installing Cowl consists of creating the right folders and moving the right files. Instead of writing a long and boring list we will proved you with this (interactive) checklist.

After

When you are done, your directory structure should be the following:

  • www-folder/ (you are now here)
    • .htaccess
    • index.php
    • app/
      • front/
      • back/
      • static/
    • cowl/
      • .git/ (the .git-directory for Cowl)
      • Cowl-related files and directories
